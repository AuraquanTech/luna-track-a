# ChatGPT App Store Listing (draft)

**Name:** Luna — Relationship Chief-of-Staff

**One-liner:** Get your psycholinguistic dating archetype in 60 seconds. Spot blind spots. Plan better dates — anywhere you date.

**Description:**
Luna isn’t a dating app and she’s not a matchmaker. She’s your relationship chief-of-staff: a practical coach that helps you date better on Hinge, Bumble, Tinder, Instagram, or real life.

What you can do:
- **Lite Archetype (30s text quiz):** a sharp, shareable archetype card.
- **Deep Archetype (60s voice transcript):** patterns, blind spots, and what actually works for you.
- **DateOps:** give your constraints and get criteria + invite scripts + a backup plan (no fake venue names).

**Privacy:** No audio stored. No venue names invented. Opt-out available.
